Start
|
v
Read string n
|
v
Initialize mod = 1000000007, total = 0, multiplier = 1, currentSum = 0
|
v
For i = n.Length - 1 down to 0
|
v
digit = n[i] - '0'
|
v
currentSum = (currentSum + digit * multiplier * (i + 1)) % mod
|
v
multiplier = (multiplier * 10 + 1) % mod
|
v
End loop
|
v
total = currentSum % mod
|
v
Output total
|
v
End

The substrings function computes the sum of all numeric substrings of a given number string n, modulo 
10+7 10 9+7. It iterates from right to left, updating a running sum of contributions from each digit. Each digitâ€™s contribution depends on its position and a multiplier that represents how many substrings include that digit in different places.
