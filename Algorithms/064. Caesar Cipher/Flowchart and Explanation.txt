The program encodes a string using the Caesar cipher by shifting each letter by a specified integer k. Uppercase and lowercase letters are shifted within their respective alphabet ranges using modular arithmetic, while non-letter characters remain unchanged. The shifted characters are collected into a StringBuilder and returned as the resulting encrypted string.
