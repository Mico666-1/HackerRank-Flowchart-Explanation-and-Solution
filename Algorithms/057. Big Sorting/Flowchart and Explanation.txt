The bigSorting function sorts a list of numeric strings by two criteria: length first and then lexicographically for equal lengths. Sorting by length ensures that shorter numbers appear before longer numbers (like 2 before 13), while lexicographic sorting resolves ties without converting strings to numeric types, which avoids issues with very large numbers. This efficiently handles extremely large numbers represented as strings.
