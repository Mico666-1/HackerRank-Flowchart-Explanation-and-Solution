Flipping all bits of a 32-bit unsigned integer n is equivalent to subtracting n from 2^32 - 1 (which is 4294967295). This works because the 32-bit range is [0, 4294967295], and flipping each 0 to 1 and 1 to 0 yields the complement within this range.
