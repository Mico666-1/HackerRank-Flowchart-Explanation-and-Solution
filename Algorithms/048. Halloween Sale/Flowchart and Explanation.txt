The howManyGames function calculates how many games can be bought with a given budget s. Starting with price p, it repeatedly deducts the current price from the budget and increments a counter. After each purchase, the game price decreases by d but never goes below m. The process continues until the remaining budget is less than the current game price, and the function returns the total number of games bought.
