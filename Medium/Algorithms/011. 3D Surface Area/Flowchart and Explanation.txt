 v
Read dimensions H and W
 |
 v
Read 2D grid A
 |
 v
Initialize area = 0
 |
 v
For each cell (i, j)
 |
 v
If A[i][j] > 0
 |
 v
Add 2 for top and bottom
 |
 v
Compute exposed sides:
front, back, left, right
 |
 v
Add exposed sides to area
 |
 v
Repeat for all cells
 |
 v
Output total area
 |
 v
End

The algorithm calculates the total surface area of stacked cubes on a grid. Each stack contributes top and bottom areas (2), plus exposed sides based on the difference between its height and neighboring stacks. Boundary stacks expose full sides.
